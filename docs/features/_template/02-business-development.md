# 02 - Business Development / Commercial Impact

## Purpose

Confirm the request makes sense strategically for Twinzy. The game is free by invariant — there is no pricing, billing, or upsell dimension — so "commercial impact" here means growth, engagement, brand trust, sponsor commitments, and cost (e.g., AI provider usage).

1. Identify the target customer, account, market, or internal business segment.
2. Explain the commercial or strategic value.
3. Review contract, SLA, pricing, or partnership impact.
4. Define rollout audience.
5. Evaluate adoption and enablement risk.
