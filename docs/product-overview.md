# Product Overview

Twinzy is a free, playful, mobile-first game. A user uploads one photo, consents, and receives:
1) the 15 visible traits the AI read (hair, eyes, face shape, ...), and 2) up to 4 playful
public style/vibe matches with a 0-100 style/vibe fit score, verdict, reasons, and a permanent
disclaimer. The game never identifies anyone, never compares biometrics, and never stores the
photo. There are no payments and no accounts.
